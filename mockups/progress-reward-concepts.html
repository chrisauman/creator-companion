Progress reward — replacing the streak dots

Five concepts for what could replace the small cyan streak dots under "Current streak". Each one rewards progress and creates a sense of momentum. Same stats strip otherwise. Pick a favorite (or a hybrid).

A. Milestone progress bar

Quietly shows how close you are to your next milestone reward. The bar fills as you write each day. When a milestone is hit, the bar resets and the label updates to the next one.

B. Tier badge

A small earned-tier badge below the streak. Tiers could be tied to your existing milestone system — Spark (3+ days), Flame (7+), Blaze (14+), Wildfire (30+), etc. Reads as a status symbol, like a quiet little trophy.
