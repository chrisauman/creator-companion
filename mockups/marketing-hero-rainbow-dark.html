Marketing hero — subtle rainbow on dark

Eight directions for a quietly multi-coloured background on the existing dark hero. All keep cyan as the dominant brand colour, layer in indigo / violet / pink / amber at low opacity, and run on slow loops (14–60s). Heavy blurs keep colours as atmosphere — never as discrete graphic shapes — so the headline always wins. Respects prefers-reduced-motion.
