FAQ

Common questions

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. The free plan has no time limit and no credit card required. You can use Creator Companion for free for as long as you want. We only ask you to pay if you want the extra features on the paid plan.

What happens to my data if I cancel my paid plan?

Your account reverts to the free plan at the end of your billing period — nothing is deleted. All your entries, photos, and journals stay exactly as they are. You'll just be limited to the free plan's limits going forward.

Why is there a 100-word limit on the free plan?

We designed the free plan around the core habit: showing up and writing something every day. 100 words is plenty for a meaningful daily check-in. If you want to write longer entries — detailed project notes, deep reflections, full creative sessions — that's what the paid plan is for.

What is backfilling, and why is it a paid feature?

Backfilling lets you write an entry for a past date — up to 2 days ago. This is useful for busy days when you didn't get a chance to write. Because backfilling interacts with your streak, we've kept it as a paid feature to maintain the integrity of the habit-building system.

What is streak pause?

Life happens. The streak pause feature lets paid users pause their streak for up to 10 days per month — for vacations, illness, or anything else — without losing their progress. You set the pause dates in advance and your streak picks up right where it left off.

Can I use Creator Companion on my phone?

Yes. Creator Companion is a progressive web app, which means you can install it on your iPhone or Android home screen directly from your browser — no app store required. On iPhone, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, tap the menu → Install App. It opens full screen and feels like a native app.

Is my journal private?

Completely. There are no social features, no sharing, no public profiles. Your entries are private by default and only you can see them. We don't read your entries or use them for any purpose other than displaying them back to you.

Can I export my data?

Yes, always — on both the free and paid plans. You can download all your entries as a JSON file (great for developers or archiving) or as a plain text file (readable anywhere). Your data always belongs to you.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're unhappy for any reason within the first 7 days of your paid plan, contact us and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe. Your payment details are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card information.
