FAQ

Common questions

How does the free trial work?

Every new account gets full access to every feature for 10 days. No credit card required to start. Three days before your trial ends, we'll email you a reminder. After 10 days, you'll need an active subscription to keep logging entries — your existing entries stay readable while you decide.

What happens when my trial ends?

If you subscribe before your trial ends, the transition is seamless — keep logging without missing a day. If you don't subscribe, your account locks for new writes but everything you've already logged stays safe. Subscribe later and pick up exactly where you left off.

What does the subscription cost?

$5/month or $50/year (save $10 with the annual plan). Both options include every feature. Cancel any time from your account page — no fees, no questions.

What is Streak Pause?

Life happens. Streak Pause lets you pause your daily streak for up to 10 days per month — for vacation, illness, or anything else — without losing your progress. Your streak picks up right where it left off when you return.

What is backfilling?

Backfilling lets you write an entry for a past date — up to 2 days back. Useful for busy days when you didn't get a chance to log. If you missed yesterday, the in-app threatened-streak banner walks you straight into a backfilled entry to save your streak.

What is Daily Spark?

A curated insight that appears on your dashboard each day. The library has 290+ hand-picked entries across creativity, resistance, discipline, deep work, originality, fear, long-term mastery, and more. Think of it as a daily nudge from the best thinking on the creative life — rotating through the full library with no repeats until you've seen them all.

Can I use Creator Companion on my phone?

Yes. Creator Companion is a progressive web app (PWA) — install it on your iPhone or Android home screen directly from your browser, no app store required. On iPhone, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, tap the menu → Install App.

Is my journal private?

Completely. No social features, no sharing, no public profiles. Your entries are private by default and only you can see them. We don't read your entries or use them for any purpose other than displaying them back to you.

Can I export my data?

Yes, always. You can download all your entries as JSON (great for developers or archiving) or plain text (readable anywhere). Your data always belongs to you.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're unhappy for any reason within the first 7 days of a paid plan, contact us and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe. Your payment details are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card information.
